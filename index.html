Welcome to the website for the 2018 Austin, Texas Bid for the Alpha Phi Omega National Convention.

Austin is bidding for the 2018 APO National Convention. Austin is a great city that offers a diverse and safe atmosphere that would make it a great host city for the convention. There are more things for students to do and places to eat than almost any other city in the country in the downtown area. Austin's unique and authentic feel resembles the things that make our fraternity the greatest in the world. The last time the convention was in Austin was in 1958.
